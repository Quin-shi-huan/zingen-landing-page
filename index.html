Know the app

The perfect app for learning to sing

Our Artificial Intelligence algorithm allows you to remove the original voice and keep the song melodies. This way, we have the largest musical repertoire for Karaoke! Furthermore, when singing, your voice and pitch are compared by the algorithm, allowing an accurate assessment of your performance, helping you improve your singing.

The largest karaoke song library

Our AI removes the voice from any song and leaves it ready to sing, just like magic!

Gamified experience

Your performance is scored according to our ultra-precise algorithm. Challenge yourself, gain recognition and celebrate your evolution!

Record your performances

Whether you want to share with friends or track your progress, our audio and video recording feature lets you get the most out of the experience!

Share with the community

Share your recordings with the community, interact and explore other people's vocal performances in your feed!

Real-time lyrics

Never miss a beat with song lyrics synced and displayed in real time. You can also turn on the song's original voice to help.
